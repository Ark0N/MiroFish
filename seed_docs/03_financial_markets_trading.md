# Energy Financial Markets, Trading Houses, and Price Benchmarks (March 2026)

## Commodity Exchanges and Benchmarks

### Oil Benchmarks
- **Brent Crude** (ICE Futures Europe, London): The global oil benchmark, administered by ICE (Intercontinental Exchange, CEO Jeffrey Sprecher). Brent is based on production from the North Sea fields (Brent, Forties, Oseberg, Ekofisk, Troll — collectively BFOET). Platts (S&P Global Commodity Insights, President Saugata Saha) publishes Dated Brent, the physical price assessment.
- **WTI (West Texas Intermediate)** (NYMEX/CME Group): CEO Terry Duffy runs CME Group, parent of NYMEX. WTI delivered at Cushing, Oklahoma — the pipeline crossroads of America, operated by multiple tank farm operators including Enbridge, Enterprise Products, Plains All American Pipeline (CEO Willie Chiang), and Magellan (now ONEOK).
- **Dubai/Oman Crude** (DME — Dubai Mercantile Exchange): CEO Owain Johnson. Oman crude futures are the primary benchmark for Asian-delivered Middle Eastern oil. S&P Global Platts also publishes Dubai assessments.
- **Shanghai Crude Oil Futures** (Shanghai International Energy Exchange, INE): RMB-denominated contracts gaining traction among Chinese refiners. Shanghai Futures Exchange (SHFE) oversees.
- **Argus Media**: CEO Adrian Binks. Publishes Argus Sour Crude Index (ASCI) — used by Saudi Aramco to price US-bound crude since 2010. Also publishes Argus European Crude Report and Argus Naphtha.
- **Energy Intelligence**: Publisher of Petroleum Intelligence Weekly (PIW) and Oil Daily. CEO Alex Koot.

### Gas Benchmarks
- **TTF (Title Transfer Facility)**: The European gas benchmark, traded on ICE Endex. Operated by Gasunie Transport Services. TTF surged to $100/MMBtu equivalent in August 2022 and has since normalized to $10-14/MMBtu range.
- **Henry Hub** (NYMEX/CME): The US natural gas benchmark, delivered at the Sabine Pipe Line interconnection in Erath, Louisiana. Sabine Pipe Line LLC (Chevron subsidiary).
- **JKM (Japan Korea Marker)**: S&P Global Platts-assessed LNG spot price for Northeast Asia. The premium Asian benchmark.
- **NBP (National Balancing Point)**: UK gas benchmark, traded on ICE. Administered by National Grid ESO (CEO Fintan Slye).
- **AECO** (Alberta): Canadian gas benchmark, key for western Canadian producers. Operated by the Alberta Natural Gas Exchange (NGX, TMX Group subsidiary, CEO Loch Adamson).
- **Asia Gas Market**: Singapore Exchange (SGX, CEO Loh Boon Chye) launched SLInG (Singapore LNG Index Group) contracts.
- **ICIS (Independent Commodity Intelligence Services)**: President Matt Brown publishes European gas price indices (ICIS Heren TTF, ICIS LNG Edge).

### Coal Benchmarks (Connected Market)
- **Newcastle Coal** (ICE Futures): Australian thermal coal benchmark, critical for Asian power generation.
- **API2/API4**: European coal indices published by Argus Media and IHS Markit (now S&P Global).
- **globalCOAL**: Platform for physical coal trading, CEO Martin Sherwood.

## Energy Trading Houses

### Commodity Trading Giants
- **Vitol Group**: CEO Russell Hardy leads the world's largest independent oil trader by volume (~8 million bbl/d). Vitol's trading desks cover crude, products, LNG, power, carbon, and metals. Vitol's VTTI terminal subsidiary (CEO Rob Gillespie) operates tank farms globally. Vitol Asia managed from Singapore by Mike Muller (head of Asia). Vitol Chartering handles shipping. Vitol Investment Partnership compensates senior traders.
- **Trafigura Group**: CEO Jeremy Weir leads the second-largest commodity trader. Trafigura's oil & petroleum products chief Hadi Hallouche manages crude and products trading. Trafigura's Puma Energy subsidiary (CEO Pierre Eladari) operates downstream retail in 35+ countries. Trafigura Mining (CEO Kostas Bintas, departed) handled metals. Nala Renewables JV with IFM Investors.
- **Glencore**: CEO Gary Nagle leads the Swiss diversified mining and commodity trading giant. Glencore's oil trading chief Alex Beard (retired, succeeded by Jyothish George) manages ~4 million bbl/d of trading volume. Glencore Energy UK handles North Sea trading. Glencore Agriculture (now Viterra, being acquired by Bunge Limited CEO Greg Heckman). Glencore's coal division is the world's largest thermal coal exporter.
- **Gunvor Group**: CEO Torbjorn Tornqvist (co-founded with Gennady Timchenko, who divested). Gunvor's crude desk in Geneva trades ~2.5 million bbl/d. Gunvor Singapore (Asia head Rahul Chadha). Gunvor's Rotterdam tank farm and Indian refinery investments.
- **Mercuria Energy Group**: CEO Marco Dunand leads this Geneva-based trader active in crude, LNG, carbon credits, and crypto trading. Mercuria's LNG desk grew from 5 to 25+ mtpa of trading volume. Mercuria Asset Holdings manages upstream investments. CFO Guillaume Vermersch.
- **Castleton Commodities International (CCI)**: CEO William Reed III. US-focused gas and power trading, operates natural gas storage assets.
- **Freepoint Commodities**: CEO David Messer. Spin-off from Louis Dreyfus focusing on North American natural gas and power.
- **Hartree Partners**: CEO Stephen Hendel. Gas, power, and carbon trading from New York and London.
- **Trailstone Group**: CEO Matthew Howard. European gas and power trading, virtual power plant optimization.
- **Macquarie Group Commodities**: CEO Shemara Wikramanayake leads Australia's largest investment bank with a top-10 global commodity trading business. Macquarie Commodities & Global Markets (CGM) headed by Nicholas O'Kane.
- **Koch Industries / Koch Supply & Trading**: CEO Charles Koch (family-owned). Koch Fertilizers, Flint Hills Resources (refining), and Koch Minerals & Trading (CEO Brad Razook).

### Oil Major Trading Arms
- **Shell Trading**: Part of Shell's Integrated Gas & Upstream division. One of the world's largest physical and financial energy traders, ~10 million bbl/d equivalent. Managed by Steve Hill (EVP trading).
- **BP Trading**: Carol Alexander leads one of the top-3 oil trading operations globally, headquartered in London with major desks in Singapore, Houston, and Chicago.
- **TotalEnergies Trading**: Part of Integrated LNG division. Active in crude, products, LNG, and power markets across European and Asian hubs.
- **Equinor Trading**: London and Stavanger desks, managed under Marketing, Midstream & Processing. Irene Rummelhoff oversees.

### Asian National Trading Companies
- **Unipec** (Sinopec trading subsidiary): CEO Chen Bo. China's largest crude oil importer, handling 3+ million bbl/d. Desks in Houston, London, and Singapore.
- **PetroChina International** (CNPC trading): Beijing/Singapore desks handle crude, products, and LNG procurement for China's largest oil company.
- **Chinaoil** (CNPC subsidiary): Crude oil and gas trading, CEO Du Peng.
- **CNOOC Trading**: Managing LNG and condensate procurement for CNOOC's downstream plants.
- **Pertamina International Trading**: Subsidiary handling Indonesian crude and product trade flows.
- **Indian Oil Corporation Marketing Division**: CEO Shrikant Madhav Vaidya manages India's largest refiner's crude procurement from Middle East, Russia (via ports of Novorossiysk, Primorsk, and Kozmino), West Africa, and Americas.

## Investment Banks and Energy Finance

Goldman's energy research publishes influential oil/gas price forecasts. Goldman Sachs Commodity Index (GSCI) is a major benchmark.
- **Morgan Stanley**: CEO Ted Pick (succeeded James Gorman). Commodities head Nancy King manages crude oil, gas, and power trading. Morgan Stanley Capital International (MSCI) publishes energy sector indices.
- **JPMorgan Chase**: CEO Jamie Dimon. Global commodities head Shawn Fay manages a $1B+ revenue oil and gas trading operation. JPM publishes influential energy market research under Natasha Kaneva (head of global commodities strategy).
- **Citigroup**: CEO Jane Fraser. Energy trading head Ed Morse (chief economist, recently retired, succeeded by Francesco Martoccia). Citi's commodities research team is among Wall Street's most-followed.
- **Bank of America**: CEO Brian Moynihan. BofA Global Research commodity strategist Francisco Blanch publishes oil/gas forecasts followed by institutional investors.
- **Barclays**: CEO C.S. Venkatakrishnan. Barclays commodities research director Amarpreet Singh covers oil/gas.
- **Deutsche Bank**: CEO Christian Sewing. Deutsche commodities head manages European gas and power trading.
- **UBS**: CEO Sergio Ermotti. UBS Commodity Strategy publishes metals and energy outlook.
- **Standard Chartered**: CEO Bill Winters. StanChart's commodity trading desk focuses on Asian energy flows. Analyst Paul Horsnell leads energy research.
